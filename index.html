Our Mission

Ardün is about healing the land and building for the future. We focus on ecological restoration, sustainable food production, and innovation that respects both people and the planet.

  • Regenerative Greenhouses – Using advanced environmental control, we simulate conditions to help plants grow stronger and more resilient.
  • Community Impact – Half of what we grow supports local communities; the other half sustains the project.
  • Learning & Experimentation – Every greenhouse is a living laboratory for soil health and ecosystem restoration.

Why Indiana?

We chose Indiana because of its rich agricultural history and its huge potential for regenerative practices that can model the Midwest.
